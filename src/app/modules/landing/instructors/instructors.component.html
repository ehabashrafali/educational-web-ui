Instructor
Aya fouad is a passionate teacher with 4+ years of experience. She is completing her Qur’an Ijazah and is qualified to teach the Qur’an Reading Course (Al-Qira’ah). Osama El‑Bosili
Instructor
Osama El‑Bosili is the founder of the school as well as an Arabic instructor for non‑native speakers. He holds an Ijazah (license) in Tajwīd granted by Shaykh Hasan Abū Shādī, head of the Al‑Masʿarawī sanad (chains of transmission) department. Osama earned a Bachelor’s degree in Arabic Language & Literature from the Islamic University of Minnesota, and he also holds an Ijazah in the Qur’an.
Hagar Ayman
Instructor
